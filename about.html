About Brooks Photonics

Brooks Photonics was founded by an expert in HgCdTe and II–VI photodetectors. We deliver physics‑first consulting that transforms photodetector data into actionable insights.

Our mission is to help research labs, companies and startups optimize performance using data they already have. We specialize in performance analysis, noise modeling, process interpretation and test strategies across HgCdTe and II‑VI materials.
