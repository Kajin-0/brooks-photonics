About Brooks Photonics

Brooks Photonics was founded by an expert in HgCdTe infrared photoconductors. With decades of experience in research, development and commercialization of long-wavelength infrared detectors, our founder has contributed to numerous innovations in detector technology.

Our mission is to provide specialized consulting services that empower companies to harness the full potential of HgCdTe photoconductors for applications ranging from defense and security to space astronomy and environmental sensing.

We pride ourselves on delivering objective, high-impact advice backed by deep technical expertise and industry insight.
