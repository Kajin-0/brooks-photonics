Model equations and limits

Bandgap

Hansen HgCdTe relation with composition x and temperature T. Cutoff is estimated from λc = 1.23984/Eg.

Photoconductive gain

Gain is estimated from carrier lifetime divided by transit time, with transit time based on length, mobility, and bias.

Frequency response

Detector response is treated as a single pole with f3dB = 1/(2πτ). Signal amplitude is corrected at the selected chopper frequency.

Noise

Total noise combines Johnson, shot, 1/f, GR, preamplifier voltage noise, and preamplifier current noise in quadrature.

NEP and D*

NEP is the total input-referred voltage noise divided by voltage responsivity. D* uses the illuminated detector area in cm².

Advisory limits

Warnings use electric field, current density, and power density. They are engineering flags, not universal material limits.
