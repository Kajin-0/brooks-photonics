Our Services

We offer a range of consulting services that require minimal startup capital yet deliver maximum impact. These services can be tailored to meet the needs of organizations at various stages of development.

  • Technical Consulting & Feasibility Studies: Evaluate the suitability of HgCdTe photoconductors for your application. Receive design recommendations and roadmaps without investing in expensive prototypes.
  • Device Modeling & Simulation: Predict the performance of HgCdTe detectors under various operating conditions using state‑of‑the‑art numerical models.
  • Training & Workshops: Educate your team through virtual or on‑site workshops on topics such as material growth, device physics, noise mechanisms and readout integration.
  • Proposal Development & Review: Strengthen funding proposals or technical papers by leveraging our experience in writing and reviewing research plans and proposals.
  • Process Consultation: Provide guidance on vendor selection, process optimization and quality control to help you establish or improve manufacturing with limited up‑front investment.
