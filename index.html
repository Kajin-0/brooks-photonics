Why Choose Us?

Brooks Photonics provides independent, physics-based analysis of infrared photodetectors, with a focus on HgCdTe, InSb, and related II-VI materials.

We help teams correctly interpret electrical, spectral, and noise data to identify true performance limits, avoid misattribution, and determine next experimental or process steps.
